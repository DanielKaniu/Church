PRAYER

Have a prayer need? Our reverend, pastors and leaders are here to pray with you over a voice/video call. Our online prayer room on Zoom* will be available every Sunday at the end of the following services:

  • 8.30am online service
  • 11.30am online service
  • 2.30pm online service
    •

You can access the prayer room on Sundays via the link on our Online English Services page or the link in the description of our online services on our YouTube channel. (Note: You do not need to create a Zoom account to be part of this session.) If you prefer to write in, you can fill in the form below with your prayer request. Alternatively, you can call our pastoral staff at 6338 4555 (Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm).

COUNSELLING

You Are Not Alone
If you are hurting and need to talk to someone, our counsellors are here to encourage you with the Word of God and to point you to Jesus. Call our Pastoral Care Helpline at 6338 4555 during office hours (Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm) to make an appointment to meet with our counsellors.
